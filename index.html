About Me

I am a highly skilled financial professional with over 20 years of experience in internal audit, financial planning & analysis, and risk management. I possess CIA and CFE certifications and have completed advanced training in risk management. My expertise in data science and analytics has enabled me to drive business insights and facilitate strategic decision-making. I am proficient in data analysis, machine learning, and data visualization, with a keen interest in uncovering hidden patterns and trends. I am currently pursuing a Master's in Information and Data Science from UC Berkeley to supplement my practical experience.
